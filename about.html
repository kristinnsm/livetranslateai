About LiveTranslateAI

Our Mission

LiveTranslateAI breaks down language barriers in real-time, enabling seamless communication for global teams, educators, healthcare providers, and businesses worldwide.

What We Do

We provide AI-powered real-time voice translation for video calls and audio conversations. Using cutting-edge OpenAI technology (Whisper STT, GPT-4o-mini, and TTS), we translate speech between 17+ languages instantly, with HD quality and minimal latency.

Our Technology

Built on modern web technologies:

  • Backend: FastAPI + WebSockets for real-time communication
  • Frontend: Vanilla JavaScript for fast, reliable performance
  • AI: OpenAI's Whisper, GPT, and TTS APIs
  • Infrastructure: Render, Netlify, and Neon PostgreSQL

Privacy First

We believe privacy is fundamental. That's why:

  • Audio is processed in real-time and never stored permanently
  • All data transmission is encrypted (HTTPS)
  • We comply with GDPR and respect your data rights
  • No third-party tracking or data selling
