## Design Specifications

### Typography
- **Font**: Inter (or Roboto/Montserrat if Inter unavailable)
- **Headline size**: 42-48px, weight 700
- **Subtext size**: 24-28px, weight 500
- **CTA size**: 32-36px, weight 600

### Colors
- **Primary text**: White (#FFFFFF)
- **Accent text**: #F59E0B (your brand accent)
- **Background**: Semi-transparent black overlay (rgba(0,0,0,0.6)) behind text

### Effects
- **Text shadow**: Black, 2px blur, 50% opacity
- **Animation**: Fade in (0.3s) → Hold → Fade out (0.3s)
- **Position**: Safe zones (avoid edges, keep in center/lower third)

### Timing Notes
- Each overlay should be visible for 2-4 seconds minimum
- Allow 0.5s fade transitions between overlays
- Total video: ~60 seconds
- Keep text on screen long enough to read twice

## Pro Tips
1. **Don't overcrowd**: Max 2 text overlays visible at once
2. **Readability**: Test on mobile - text should be readable at small sizes
3. **Brand consistency**: Use your brand colors (#0891B2 primary, #F59E0B accent)
4. **Call-to-action**: End with clear CTA (website URL)
5. **Pacing**: Let the demo breathe - don't rush text changes
