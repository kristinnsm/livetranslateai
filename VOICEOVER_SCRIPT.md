## Delivery Tips

### Pacing
- **Normal pace**: ~150 words per minute
- **Slightly faster**: ~160-170 words (more energetic)
- **Slower**: ~130 words (more deliberate, professional)

### Tone
- **Conversational**, not robotic
- **Confident** but friendly
- **Enthusiastic** about the product
- **Clear** pronunciation (especially "LiveTranslateAI dot com")

### Emphasis Points
- "**incredibly simple**" (Scene 1)
- "**17 interface languages**" (Scene 2)
- "**under one second**" (Scene 5)
- "**LiveTranslateAI dot com**" (Scene 6 - CTA)

### Pauses
- Brief pause after "LiveTranslateAI" (first mention)
- Pause before "No problem" (Scene 6)
- Natural pauses between scenes

---

## Recording Tips

1. **Environment**: Quiet room, minimal echo
2. **Mic**: Phone mic is fine, but speak clearly
3. **Energy**: Sound excited but professional
4. **Practice**: Read through 2-3 times first
5. **Multiple takes**: Record each scene separately, edit together
6. **Breathing**: Take a breath between scenes
