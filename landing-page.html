Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the translation? +
Our translations are powered by OpenAI's GPT-4 and Whisper AI, achieving 85-95% accuracy depending on language pair and audio quality. We handle accents, technical terms, and conversational language exceptionally well.
Is my conversation private? +
Absolutely. We use in-memory processing only - no conversations are recorded or stored on our servers. All audio is processed in real-time and immediately discarded. Your privacy is our #1 priority.
What devices can I use? +
LiveTranslateAI works on any device with a modern web browser - desktop, laptop, tablet, or smartphone. Chrome is recommended for the best experience, but Firefox, Safari, and Edge are also supported.
How fast is the translation? +
Translation latency is typically under 1 second in realtime mode and 3-5 seconds in traditional mode. This means near-instant communication, allowing for natural conversation flow.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes! No contracts or commitments. Cancel your subscription anytime with one click. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
Do you offer refunds? +
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If you're not satisfied for any reason, we'll refund your entire payment.
How does the free 30 minutes work? +
Sign up with your email (no credit card needed) and you'll get 30 minutes of free translation time. This is enough for 1-2 real calls to test the quality and see if it works for your needs. No tricks, no auto-charges.
What happens after I use my 30 free minutes? +
After 30 minutes, you can start a 7-day unlimited trial by adding a credit card. You won't be charged during the trial. If you love it, you'll automatically continue at $19/month. If not, cancel anytime during the 7 days with one click.
Why does the trial require a credit card? +
The 7-day trial requires a card to prevent abuse and ensure serious users get the best experience. You already got 30 free minutes to test - the trial unlocks unlimited use for a week. You can cancel anytime before being charged.
