## **EMERGENCY TROUBLESHOOTING**

**If you're not getting signups:**
- Post more frequently (2x per day instead of 1x)
- Add more emotion to your story
- Respond to EVERY comment immediately
- Try different subreddits

**If you're getting signups but no conversions:**
- Your 15 min trial might be too short (test 30 min)
- Add live chat to landing page
- Offer extended trial for feedback
- Make pricing more visible

**If you're burning out:**
- Batch content (write Week 2 content on Sunday)
- Use ChatGPT to generate variations
- Take 1 day off per week (Sunday)
- Remember: This is a sprint, not a marathon
